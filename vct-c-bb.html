Blackboard Collaborate issues

-Connection Error: Spinning Purple Wheel

If you are trying to connect to a session and, instead of loading, you see a spinning purple wheel, check your cookie settings.

-Connection Error: A03 Connection Error

An A03 Connection Error typically means that the session was not found or the token not valid. Please ask the moderator to resend the guest link.
